I've been experimenting with tritone substitutions and drop-2 voicings to breathe some new life into *Autumn Leaves*. 

Normally, the A section follows a very standard `ii - V - I - IV - vii° - III7 - vi` progression. 

Instead of the standard `Am7 - D7 - Gmaj7`, I tried substituting the `D7` with an `Ab7` (its tritone). The resulting bassline `A -> Ab -> G` moves entirely in chromatic descending steps, creating a incredibly smooth, melancholic voice leading that fits the theme of the song perfectly.

I also mapped out a few spread voicings for the left hand to free up the right hand for more melodic embellishment. Going to try recording a rough take of this arrangement this weekend.
