ingredients:
  - "1 cup unsalted butter (browned and cooled)"
  - "1 cup dark brown sugar, packed"
  - "1/2 cup granulated white sugar"
  - "2 large eggs (room temperature)"
  - "2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour"
  - "1 tsp baking soda"
  - "1 tsp flaky sea salt"
  - "2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chunks"

Browning the butter is the absolute most important step here. It completely transforms the flavor profile from a standard, overly sweet cookie into something that tastes like it came from a high-end bakery.

**Instructions:**
1. Brown the butter over medium heat until it foams and you see brown specks at the bottom. Pour into a bowl and let it cool for 15 minutes.
2. Whisk the cooled brown butter with both sugars until smooth.
3. Beat in the eggs one at a time.
4. Fold in the dry ingredients until just combined. Do not overmix.
5. Fold in the chocolate chunks.
6. Chill the dough for at least 30 minutes.
7. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10-12 minutes.
8. Top with flaky sea salt immediately out of the oven.
